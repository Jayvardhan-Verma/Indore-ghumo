Adventure and Resorts

Here are some famous Adventure and Resorts in indore which you should visit.

Simcha

Escape the summer heat at Indore's beloved Adventure Park, SIMCHA. Every moment is a lively escapade – hop, jump, spring, swim, run – a new game, a fresh quest awaits with over 60 activities. Everything you’ve dreamt of, from a giant swing to a toy train, rope courses, and trampolines, it’s all here. Dive into these enchanting wonders; explore more or simply join us for an adventure-filled experience!.

Location -

Near, Ahead of Sonway Toll Plaza, NH3, opp. flying junction petrol pump, Rau, Indore, Madhya Pradesh 453331

Grand Machal

The Grand Machal is a kaleidoscope of natural wonders, adventures, sports, resort, and eateries. TGM is a man-made jungle that provides tranquilly to individuals seeking a break from their daily routine and work burden. TGM's major goal is to provide "Peace & Joy" to our visitors, as our slogan states. Whether it’s a romantic getaway or family time, a corporate event or a destination wedding, we have all what you’re looking for. Come experience the largest adventure park of Central India.

Location -

The Grand Machal NH47, Indore - Dhar Rd, Machal, Madhya Pradesh

Nakhrali Dhani

Nakhrali Dhani Resort is an excellent choice for travellers visiting Indore, offering a family-friendly environment alongside many helpful amenities designed to enhance your stay. During your stay, take advantage of some of the amenities offered, including 24 hour front desk, a concierge, and room service. Nakhrali Dhani is a renowned village resort in Indore which presents the folk culture along with all the traditional events of Rajasthan.

Location -

Rau Road, Nakhrali Dhani Bypass, Opp. Railway Crossing, Indore, Madhya Pradesh

Safari Park

safari - A Beautiful Isolated Adventure Park, ideal for all walks of life, i.e. Family, Corporate, Schools or youth, we have a special program for all. One can enjoys various adventure Activities, Can Stay in beautiful tents, Camp Fire, experiencing Team Building and theme based programs. A variety of adventures are available here, including artificial mountaineering and mud sports, rope climbing, pools and gardens.


Location -

9 MSJ Estate, Lasudia Parmar, Dakachya, Indore, Madhya Pradesh

Crescent Waterpark

Crescent Water Park is a well-known water park in Indore, India. It has a variety of water-based attractions and activities for people of all ages. The park has thrilling water slides, wave pools, lazy rivers, and splash pads, making it a pleasant place for families and parties. Crescent Water Park is well-known for organising events and parties, making it a popular venue for birthday parties and corporate outings.

Location -

Gram Jamniya, Near Narmada Kshipra Devguradia Khurd, Kampel Rd, Indore, Madhya Pradesh
